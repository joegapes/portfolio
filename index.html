Unity Game Developer

I'm 15 years old and originally started making games in Roblox Studio. I was pretty good at building and designing cool stuff, but I always felt kind of boxed in. That all changed after I discovered Gorilla Tag — its unique VR locomotion blew my mind and inspired me to try something new.

Curious and motivated, I searched up “how to make a Gorilla Tag fan game” on YouTube, downloaded Unity, and jumped right in. After following a tutorial, I had my very first working VR game. The only problem? I was a complete beginner at Unity.

That's when I reached out to Gambs — absolute legend. He helped me out a ton and kept me going when things got rough. Without him, I probably would've gone back to Roblox. But instead, I pushed forward, got better at Unity, and started learning C#.

Now, I’m building VR games, heh... lets just say.. I’m just getting started.
